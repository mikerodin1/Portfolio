About Me

Hello, this is Michael.

I am a Registered Practical Nurse in Canada with a deep commitment to providing compassionate, evidence-informed, and culturally sensitive care. My nursing journey began with personal experiences that taught me empathy, resilience, and the importance of human connection.

Before nursing, I worked in business management, but caring for loved ones through illness inspired a career shift into healthcare. These moments taught me that healing is not only physical—it’s emotional and spiritual.

Today, I am focused on advancing my clinical skills, promoting holistic care, and contributing to equitable healthcare for all communities.

My Philosophy

  • Listen with empathy and intention
  • Advocate for clients’ dignity and autonomy
  • Collaborate across disciplines for better outcomes
  • Uphold professionalism, respect, and compassion
