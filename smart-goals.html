Goal 1: Strengthen Therapeutic Communication and Confidence

During my placement at Ontario Shores, I aim to build trust and connection with patients by initiating at least one therapeutic conversation per shift. Weekly feedback and reflective journaling will refine my communication skills, aligning with CNO standards for the Therapeutic Nurse-Client Relationship (CNO, 2006).
